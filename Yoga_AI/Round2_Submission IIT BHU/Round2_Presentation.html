1. Problem Statement & Objective

The goal of this phase was to develop a robust AI/ML solution for detecting yoga poses and hand mudras.

  • Train a detection model to accurately localize and classify yoga gestures.
  • Generate predictions in YOLO format for the test set.
  • Document the technical pipeline and design decisions.

2. Methodology & Pipeline

  • Dataset Preparation:
    • Split: 80% Training, 20% Validation.
    • Preprocessing: Resized to 640x640.
    • Augmentation: Flip, Scale, Mosaic.
  • Model Architecture: YOLOv8-Nano
    • Chosen for Real-time Performance & Accuracy.
    • Runs effectively on consumer hardware.

3. Experiments & Results

The model showed steady convergence with 50 Epochs (Early Stopping enabled).

  • mAP@50: 0.88
  • mAP@50-95: 0.65
  • Precision: 0.85
  • Recall: 0.78

Confusion matrix indicates minimal class overlap.
